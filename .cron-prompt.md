# 找谁玩 (Who To Play With) — WeChat Mini App Builder

## Your Mission
Build 找谁玩 incrementally — a social relationship tracker that helps users see the hidden patterns in their friendships.

## Core Philosophy
**Every friendship lives on a scatter plot:**
- **X-axis**: How often + how long you spend with someone (frequency × duration, with recency decay)
- **Y-axis**: How the hangouts actually feel (quality score, not quantity)
- **Reference line**: Y = X (balanced effort-reward line)

**The gap from Y=X is everything:**
- **Above Y=X** → You're getting more joy than effort invested. Rewarding friendship — prioritize these.
- **Below Y=X** → You're putting in more than you feel. One-sided — consider pulling back or having a real conversation.
- **On Y=X** → Balanced and sustainable.

**Duration matters**: A 30-min coffee counts differently than a full-day trip. X combines frequency AND duration.

**Recommendations based on scatter position:**
- Friend below Y=X line but rare → "You should reconnect meaningfully — these hangouts always feel great"
- Friend well below Y=X and frequent → "You may be over-investing here. Worth a check-in or pullback."
- Friend far above Y=X → "Rare gems — make time to see them more"

## Product Structure
- **Home**: Scatter plot showing all friendships as dots. Tap a dot → friend's card.
- **Friend Card**: Shows X (effort), Y (feelings), gap, recent hangouts, recommended actions.
- **Log Hangout**: Quick-log who you met, how long, and how you felt.
- **Insights**: Patterns — "You've seen X 3× this week but felt 😕 about it" or "Your highest-joy friend is Y, you saw them last month."

## Data Model
- Friend: name, avatar, tags (colleague/school/family/club)
- Hangout: friend_id, date, duration_minutes, feeling_score (1-5), notes
- Derived: recency_score, frequency_score, duration_score, feeling_avg, gap_score
